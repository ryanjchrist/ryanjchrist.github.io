I'm Ryan Christ, a senior at Duke University double majoring in Mechanical Engineering and Computer Science. Originally from Rochester, NY, I first discovered my passion for engineering while spending time in my dad's shop, where I learned to translate ideas into tangible creations.

I'm passionate about the intersection of mechanics and software, especially interdisciplinary projects that draw on both skill sets. I'm particularly interested in practical applications of machine learning in mechanical engineering contexts, autonomous systems, and hands-on prototyping.
